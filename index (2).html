About Chintan Traders

Three decades of trust, expertise and genuine safety solutions

Your safety. Our purpose.

Founded in 1996 by Chintan Kadakia in Ankleshwar, Chintan Traders has grown from a local safety equipment supplier into one of Gujarat's most trusted authorised distributors of PPE and fire safety products.

Serving the dense industrial belt of GIDC Ankleshwar and Bharuch, we supply certified personal protective equipment to chemical plants, pharmaceutical units, oil & gas facilities, construction sites and manufacturing industries.

As authorised distributors for KARAM and Venus Safety — two of India's most respected safety brands — every product we supply is genuine, fully certified and backed by manufacturer warranty. We also carry a wide portfolio of trusted brands including Dräger, DuPont, Atlas Protective and more.

Registered on the Government e-Marketplace (GeM), IEC certified for exports, and proud to have supplied to ISRO ahead of the Chandrayaan-3 mission — we bring the same commitment to every order, whether it's 10 helmets or 10,000.

🛡 Every product we supply carries a valid CE, IS or ANSI certification — making your factory audit-ready at all times.

Fall Protection

Complete height safety systems from India's leading fall protection brand

Fall protection range
KARAM authorised — India's #1 fall protection brand with 3,800+ certified products

Full Body Harnesses

Class A, B and C harnesses for general industry, construction and rescue. Quick-connect buckles, dorsal and sternal D-rings.

Lanyards

Fixed, adjustable, twin-leg and shock-absorbing lanyards. Webbing and rope variants. 1m, 1.5m, 2m lengths.

Self-Retracting Lifelines (SRL)

Compact 3m to 50m SRLs with swivel snap hooks. Fast-acting braking mechanism. Steel and galvanized wire rope.

Rope Grab & Rope Access

Rope grabs, rope access kits, descenders and ascenders for rope-based working-at-height applications.

Anchor Devices

Temporary and permanent anchor points, deadweight anchors, beam clamps and roof anchors for various structures.

Tripod & Confined Space

Aluminium and steel tripods, winches, rescue davit arms and confined space entry kits for manholes and tanks.

Horizontal Lifeline Systems

Stainless steel and galvanized wire rope horizontal lifeline systems for rooftops, bridges and gantries.

Safety Nets

Personnel safety nets, tool nets, debris nets — compliant with EN 1263. Various mesh sizes and load ratings.

Fire Safety

Certified fire protection and emergency response equipment

Fire safety range
From fire extinguishers to proximity suits — fully stocked for Gujarat's industrial belt

Fire Extinguishers

ABC dry powder, CO2, foam, water mist and clean agent extinguishers. 1kg to 75kg. BIS approved.

Fire Fighting Suits

Proximity suits, entry suits, structural firefighting suits. Aluminised and Nomex variants. EN 469 compliant.

Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA)

30-min and 60-min compressed air SCBAs for firefighting, confined space and chemical emergency response.

Fire Hose Reels & Accessories

Fire hose reels, delivery hoses, branch pipes, couplings and hydrant valves for fixed fire suppression systems.

Smoke & Gas Detectors

Smoke detectors, heat detectors, flame detectors, combustible gas detectors and CO monitors for early warning systems.

Fire Blankets

Fibreglass and wool fire blankets for kitchen, laboratory and industrial use. 1m x 1m to 2m x 2m sizes.

Emergency Escape Hoods

Self-rescue escape hoods with 15-minute protection against smoke, CO and toxic gases for building evacuation.

Fire Safety Signage

Photoluminescent and LED fire exit signs, fire action notices, evacuation maps and first aid signs — IS 9457 compliant.

Lockout Tagout (LOTO)

Complete energy isolation and control-of-hazardous-energy equipment

LOTO range
Preventing accidental equipment start-up during maintenance — OSHA and IS 5216 compliant

Lockout Padlocks

Keyed-different and keyed-alike safety padlocks. Red, blue, yellow — colour-coded by worker or department. Lightweight aluminum body.

Lockout Stations

Wall-mounted LOTO stations stocked with padlocks, hasps, tags and devices. Visible and accessible at point-of-use.

Hasps & Group Lockout

Multi-lock hasps for group lockout allowing up to 6 workers to lock simultaneously on shared energy sources.

Valve Lockouts

Ball valve, gate valve, butterfly valve and plug valve lockouts. Universal and size-specific variants in durable polypropylene.

Circuit Breaker Lockouts

MCB lockouts, plug lockouts, push-button lockouts and electrical panel lockout devices for electrical energy isolation.

Safety Tags & Labels

Danger and caution tagout tags, equipment tags, barricade tags. Tear-resistant polyester and card variants.

Cable & Pipe Lockouts

Flexible cable lockouts for irregular-shaped valves and pipe-end lockouts for isolation of pipe systems.

LOTO Kits

Ready-to-use LOTO kits with padlocks, hasps, tags and devices in a portable pouch or case — for contractors and maintenance teams.
